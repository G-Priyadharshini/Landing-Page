More Information
Anna University, Tiruchirappalli was established in January 2007. Earlier it was a part of Anna University at Chennai which had to be divided into Four Universities in different Locations for administrative convenience. It has been established by the Government of Tamil Nadu as a Technical University. The University started functioning from 01-02-2007. It is named after the former Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu, Conjeevaram Natarajan Annadurai who was popularly known as 'Anna' meaning brother in the local language!
